Projects

Over the years, I’ve had a handful of jobs. From delivering appliances, working as a stonemason, and being a waiter at a restaurant, I feel as though I've been imbued with a very wide skillset. During the summer of 2024 I had the opportunity to WWOOF in Dogliani Italy, a small agricultural town ontop of the French Riviera. During my stay with my host family, Freddy and Ursula, I learned lots about permaculture, Italian culture, and resourcesfulness. One of my projects was building a stone retaining wall and repairing hundred year old masonry work- I find it cool to think about how there is still a piece of me left in the form of that wall out in the Italian mountains. These experiences shaped how I approach problem-solving and creativity.
